### Team Synapse | Kishlay Kumar · Anup Pal · Shivam Kumar

> **Goal**: Win by showing a real, working, polished product that directly solves the problem statement — not a wireframe or mockup.

## OPENING (60 seconds) — Hook the audience

> *Stand at the login screen. Don't touch anything yet. Speak first.*

**Say:**
> "Hexaware runs hundreds of training batches every year — thousands of fresh hires, dozens of trainers, spread across multiple locations. And today, most of that is managed through spreadsheets, emails, and manual follow-ups.
>
> No one has a single view of what's happening. Ops leads spend 30–45 minutes every morning just compiling a status update. Trainers don't know which candidates are about to fall behind until it's too late. And the candidates themselves? They get almost no visibility into their own progress.
>
> We built Maverick to fix all of that."

*Pause. Let that land. Then:*

> "This is the Maverick Execution Platform — a full training management system with two portals, a real backend API, and 12 AI-powered surfaces, all working live, right now."

## SECTION 4 — Tech & Architecture Q&A (2 min)

> Use this if the panel asks technical questions.

---

**Q: How does the AI work?**
> "We use Google Gemini — specifically `gemini-2.5-flash` — via the official `@google/genai` SDK on the backend. The frontend never calls Gemini directly. Every AI feature goes through our Express API, which composes the prompt, injects live training data as context, calls Gemini, parses the response, and returns typed data to the frontend. This keeps the frontend clean, keeps our prompts secure, and means we can swap or upgrade the model without touching any UI code."

---

**Q: How do you prevent the AI from going off-topic?**
> "Two layers. First, every prompt has a shared system guardrail — we tell Gemini it is MaveAI and it only helps with training platform topics or technical study material. Second, before the message even reaches Gemini, we run a hard regex pattern filter on the backend. If the prompt matches an off-topic pattern — NSFW, politics, celebrity gossip, anything irrelevant — we refuse it instantly without spending a single token. Belt-and-braces."

---

**Q: Is the data real?**
> "The data is a realistic, deterministic mock store — seeded so it looks like real training data with realistic patterns. The architecture is production-ready: Drizzle ORM and PostgreSQL are pre-wired in the shared workspace packages, so connecting a real database is a configuration change, not a rewrite. The mock lets us demo anywhere without a database dependency."

---

**Q: Why Gemini? Why not OpenAI?**
> "We evaluated both. Gemini 2.5 Flash gives us strong structured-output support, good JSON-mode reliability, fast response times, and a generous free tier for development. The switch from OpenAI to Gemini was straightforward because all our AI calls go through a shared `aiComplete` / `aiCompleteJSON` abstraction — we only had to change the client, not a single prompt or route."

---

**Q: What would it take to go to production?**
> "Three things: wire in a real Postgres database using the Drizzle setup we already have, add authentication via Hexaware SSO (OIDC), and switch the mock data layer to real API reads. The frontend, AI layer, design system, and all the business logic are production-quality already."

## TIPS FOR WINNING

- **Don't read slides — demo the app.** Every second of live AI output is worth more than any bullet point.
- **Let the AI breathe.** When a Gemini response is generating, narrate what it's doing — "it's reading the live batch data right now…" — so silence feels intentional.
- **Show the guardrail.** Most teams won't have one. It's a differentiator and a sign of production thinking.
- **Hit the offline demo.** It's visually striking and shows you thought about edge cases.
- **If asked about cost:** "We treat every LLM call as a cost line. We cache with TanStack Query, persist chat history locally so returning users cost zero new API calls, and our guardrail cuts wasted tokens on off-topic requests."
- **If AI is slow:** "Gemini 2.5 Flash is designed for speed — this is just the round-trip to Google's servers. In a production deployment we'd add response streaming to show output as it generates."
- **Confidence is contagious.** You built something real. Own it.
